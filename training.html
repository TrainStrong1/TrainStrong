CPR Training

HeartCode BLS (American Heart Association) This course is designed for healthcare providers caring for patients both in and out of the hospital setting. This training is designed for indviduals pursuing careers in nursing, physical therapy, athletic training or other healthcare programs. HeartCode blended learning delivers quality resuscitation education regardless of where providers are located and gives them more control to complete the course at their own pace. Providers first complete the online portion of HeartCode BLS through the American Heart Association, and then complete a hands-on skills session with Train Strong AHA certified BLS Instructors.



Certification Details

HeartCode BLS (American Heart Association) The cost for the HeartCode BLS skills class is $25, which includes the certification card. The online portion of the course is completed and paid for separately through the American Heart Association. A course completion certificate is required to be eligible for the class completion. We proudly supply CPR eCards issued by the American Heart Association (AHA). Upon successful completion of your CPR certification with Train Strong, you will receive an AHA eCard. These eCards provide a convenient and secure way to verify your CPR certification. Easily accessed, shared, and verified online, trust in the AHA's commitment to quality and reliability for all your CPR certification needs. For more information, visit the American Red Cross.
