Welcome to Train Strong


Our mission is to educate the public on essential life saving skills such as CPR and first aid.


At Train Strong, we believe that every community member has the potential to save a life with the right training. Our expert instructors are dedicated to providing you with the skills and confidence needed to respond effectively in medical emergencies.

We offer certification classes on a first-come, first-served basis, so early registration is highly recommended due to high demand and limited availability of our skilled instructors. Join us and become a hero in your community!


Why choose Train Strong?


  • Expert Instructors: Learn from experienced professionals who are passionate about teaching life saving techniques.
  • Hands-On Training: Gain practical experience through interactive sessions and real life scenarios.
  • Flexible Scheduling: We offer a variety of class times to fit your busy schedule.
  • Community Impact: Empower yourself and others by spreading knowledge and preparedness.

Don't wait until it's too late. Sign up today and take the first step towards becoming a certified life-saver!
