Certification Information Sessions


The sessions will cover multiple categories, including:

  • Basic CPR: Basic CPR Learn the essential skills to perform Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) in emergency situations. This session covers chest compressions, rescue breaths, and how to use an Automated External Defibrillator (AED).

  • BLS:This comprehensive session focuses on Basic Life Support techniques, including CPR for adults, children, and infants, as well as advanced airway management and recognizing life-threatening emergencies.

  • First aid: Gain practical knowledge on how to provide immediate care for injuries and illnesses. This session includes wound care, treating burns, managing fractures, and handling medical emergencies like choking and allergic reactions.

  • Bookings: Easily schedule your training sessions with one of our staff members they will contact you with in 24 hours after you fill out the form on the Contact Us page. Choose from available dates and times, and secure your spot in our Basic CPR, BLS, or First Aid courses with just a few clicks.
